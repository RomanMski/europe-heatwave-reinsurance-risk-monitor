Heat Stress Reinsurance Workbench

I wanted this to feel like something a reinsurance team could actually open during a heat event. Not a notebook screenshot, not a toy chart. Live weather comes in, each region gets compared with its own climate history, then the page asks the insurance question: where is the stress, and does the trigger really catch it?

This is a portfolio project, so the numbers are scenario values. Still, the workflow is real enough to show the thinking: data quality, thresholds, basis risk, payout sensitivity and future heat stress.
